About Us

DR.Events.beauty.Pageant is an exciting beauty pageant that will be hosting its first season in December 2024 in Delhi, India, Participants from both India and abroad will have the opportunity to showcase their talent and grace, as they receive training from popular industry artists in grooming and preparation.
